people online
lokalpo.st lets you connect with other people who share your internet connection... [read more]
Messages posted while connected to a coffee shop's wifi are only visible to other people connected to the same wifi. Posts from your office can only be seen by others on that network, and so on. Posts appear on the page in real time, so you can use lokalpo.st as a chat room. There is an indicator in the upper right when others are online. The ten most recent posts are saved and displayed when the page loads, so you can also use lokalpo.st as a bulletin board.

Important: Don't post any private information. Your network's ip address probably changes periodically. After a change, someone who visits lokalpo.st from a different network with your old ip address will be able to see your old posts.

Built by @veatch. Source on github.
